Introduction

Welcome to String Art Generator. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and web platform.

By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Images and Media

User-Uploaded Images: When you upload an image to create string art, the image is saved and processed using our algorithms on our secure servers to generate your string art output.

Storage: We save and process these images solely for the purpose of generating your patterns. String art creations may be stored during processing but can be deleted at your request.

1.2 Device and Usage Data

We may collect technical information including device model, operating system, unique device identifiers, and app version. We also track usage patterns such as features used, nail/thread count preferences, and performance data to improve our algorithm.

1.3 Permissions

We request Camera and Gallery access only when you explicitly initiate an image upload or photo capture within the App.

No Sharing Policy

Your data is yours alone. We do not share, sell, or trade your uploaded images, generated patterns, or account information with other users or third-party companies for marketing purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Core Functionality: To process images and generate precise string art paths.
  • App Improvement: To fix bugs and optimize the processing speed of our algorithms.
  • User Experience: To remember your preferred settings and provide support.
  • Analytics: To measure performance via anonymized usage patterns.

3. Third-Party Services

We may share limited, non-personally identifiable data with trusted service providers for:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics and Firebase Analytics to understand app usage.
  • Infrastructure: Secure cloud storage providers (e.g., Google Cloud or AWS).
  • Monitoring: Crashlytics for performance tracking.

4. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You may:

  • Update or correct your settings.
  • Delete your generated string art and uploaded images.
  • Request full account erasure via our Delete Account page.

5. Legal Compliance

GDPR (EEA Users): You have the right to access, rectification, erasure, and data portability. Contact our DPO at the email below for inquiries.

CCPA (California Users): You have the right to know what information is collected and the right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, contact:

Email: privacy@stringartgenerator.com

Support: support@stringartgenerator.com

Web: www.stringartgenerator.com/privacy
